## Marinaded Chicken Thigh

| Ingredient |  |
| ----- | ----- |
| Chicken Thigh | 2kg  |
| Cumin Seeds | 4 tsp |
| Corriander Seed | 2 tsp |
| Ginger | 2cm knob |
| Garlic | 4 cloves |
| Greek Yoghurt | ~4 tblsp |
| Smoked Paprika | 2 tsp |
| Turmeric | 2 tsp |
| Ground Corriander Seed | 4 tsp |
| Lemon | 1 |
| Salt | To taste |

## Method

Toast off the cumin and corriander seeds.<br>

Grind the seeds, ginger, garlic and salt together to form a paste.<br>

Prick the chicken with a fork and squeeze the lemon over it<br>

Add in the paste, yoghurt and other dry spices and mix together<br>

>Best if you can let it marinade in the fridge for ~6hrs before use or freezing
