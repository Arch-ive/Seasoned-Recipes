# Method

Oil and season the chicken with salt and pepper and grill on the BBQ with whatever veg on hand that suits (e.g. cauliflower).

Steam the rest of the veg.

Reheat the [Romesco Sauce]({{< ref "recipe/base/romesco" >}}) 

Serve all together.
