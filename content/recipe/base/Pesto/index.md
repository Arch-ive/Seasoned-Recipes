### Pesto

| Ingredient |  |
| ----- | ----- |
| Basil |  1<sup>1/2</sup> Cups |
| Parmisan Cheese |  1/2 Cup |
| Pine Nuts |  1/4 Cup |
| Olive Oil | To losen |


Mix all up in a blender or if you have time, mortar and pestle. Have fresh or freeze for later

>These ratios may not be accurate. Pay attention next time you make it.
