| Ingredient  | Amount |
| ----- | ---- |
| Tuna in Oil | 2 cans |
| Kewpie Mayo | To mix |
| Celery | 1 stick |
| Dill | small bunch |
| Salt | to taste |

# Method

Drain one can of the oil but add the other one oil and all into a bowl. Mix in the mayo and add the dill and finely chopped celery. Pinch of salt.
