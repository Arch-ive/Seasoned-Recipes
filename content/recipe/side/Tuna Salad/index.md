| Ingredient  | Amount |
| ----- | ---- |
| Tuna in Oil | 2 cans |
| Kewpie Mayo | Base |
| Australian Mustard | To taste |
| Pickle* | 4-5 slices |
| Celery *| 1 stick |
| Dill* | Small ammount |
| Parsley* | Small ammount |
| Salt | To taste |

# Method

Drain one can of the oil but add the other one oil and all into a bowl. Mix in the mayo, mustard, herb and finely chopped celery/pickle. Pinch of salt.
